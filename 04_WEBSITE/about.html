Our Philosophy

At Siyeh Consultants we believe that technology is a useful tool that shouldn't be approachable only to a select few. Our expertise lies in our ability to enhance your experience using engineering software design to materialize your visions. What excites us is all the possibilities our collaboration can fulfill. We want you to feel empowered and emboldened about your visions so that your company can thrive.

Our workflow places deep focus on knowing the precise problems that tie down your business from reaching its full potential.Our philosophy was inspired from Mt. Siyeh located in Montana, which was a source of inspriration and admiration for our team.

The solutions we propose must be affordable, quick, and easily adaptable. the instructions for the adoption of these solutions are supplied clearly to the point. We do not believe that our job ends once we deliver our software to work with your needs. Support and maintenance are an inseparable part of the engineering design industry and we provide hassle-free support for all our services. We prioritize long-term working relationships above anything else. Providing regular updates and maintenance in a timely manner is important for us.

A big part of our success in the long term also depends on being mindful of the effects technology can have for different people. the profits and earnings from our business are used to fund our sister NGO aimed at raising poor communities in India through education and technology. A negative effect of the overly technological world is that it adversely displaces people who cannot keep up with the fast pace of changes affecting their livelihood and future. We understand this phenomenon deeply and have developed programs to ensure that our technology products and developments do not adversely affect workers. Educating people to be effective users of technology lies at the heart of our work.

About Us

Siyeah Consultants was born out of a desire to bring a change. Three friends from diverse backgrounds, felt that adapting to new processes and challenges requires significant changes at various levels in business - be it technology, innovation, management or communication. With this understanding, so we wanted to ensure we spend enough time to find out the real problems affecting you and your business and through innovative software and processes, help you in providing a better work environment for your team.
